About Us

With over 40 years of experience since 1978, we specialize in precision machining of various high-performance engineered plastic materials, purchased directly from major manufacturers. Our climate-controlled facility ensures efficient production, and we maintain a comprehensive inventory for quick turnaround on machined parts.

Our Mission

In partnership with our customers and with a commitment to our people, we of PMC are dedicated to excellence in serving our customers’ needs with world-class machined plastic parts, thereby profitably achieving the precision and exactness of all plastic machined parts produced by Paramont Machine Company.

Quality Policy

Quality Policy PMC is certified to AS9100 & ISO9000 standards. PMC’s commitment to achieving customer enthusiasm is also reflected in our mission statement and the following quality objectives:

1. Deliver world-class quality plastic parts and services on time to our customers.

2. Establish and maintain an effective, company-wide quality system.

3. Foster a teamwork and data driven approach to continuous improvement.

4. Support the empowerment of our employees through education and training.
