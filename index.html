Your Specialists in Plastics Fabrication

Paramont Machine Company has built a reputation within the plastic industry for producing quality parts with on-time delivery and competitive pricing. We are capable of holding tight tolerances and fine-machined surface finishes on plastic machined parts.

Dependable Experience

Our AS9100 & ISO9000 Certified Quality System is derived from 30 plus years experience in the plastics industry and is seen in the stringent standards we set throughout planning, production, packaging, and shipment of your components.

Affordability Meets Skill

Milling and turning operations are handled by skilled machinists with extensive experience in plastics. We can easily meet your requirements for tight tolerance machining on the most demanding materials including Torlon®, Vespel®, PEEK®, and Semitron®. In addition, our ability to purchase raw material direct from the manufacturer allows us to meet your cost competitive requirements.
