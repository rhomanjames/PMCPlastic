Industries We Serve

Paramont Machine Company specializes in machining components for diverse industries. We can assist with engineering challenges, including replacing metal parts with plastics for improved chemical resistance, weight reduction, and cost savings. As an AS9100B and ISO9001-2008 certified company, we maintain high-quality standards, supported by our state-of-the-art software, Vantage.

Industries

Aerospace & Defense

Plastic components are vital in aerospace and defense, offering weight reduction and cost advantages over metals. PMC actively collaborates with customers, seeking new materials for their applications and providing cost-competitive prices on existing components.

Chemicals, Oil & Gas

We serve the chemical processing and oil & gas industries, providing materials that meet demanding requirements. Through collaboration with customers and OEMs, we offer the latest materials, leading to improved component performance, reduced weight, increased mean time between rebuilds, and ten-fold enhanced wear resistance using advanced engineering plastics.

Fluid Power / Seals

PMC has 30+ years of experience in plastic machining for Fluid Power components, providing advanced engineering plastics with tight tolerances. We lead in new material adoption by partnering with top manufacturers, benefiting customers in Aerospace, Life Sciences, and Chemical and Oil processing industries.

Food Processing

Plastics are the preferred choice for the Food Processing industry due to their adherence to strict FDA, USDA, 3A Dairy, or NSF requirements. They outperform metals in longevity and repeated sanitizing operations. PMC machines various materials for this industry and offers assistance in specifying and suggesting new materials for your specific application.

Life Sciences

Our machined components provide complete material traceability and certifications, ensuring high-quality compliance. As direct material purchasers, we offer competitive costs and access to the latest plastic technology, assisting customers in cost-effective material selection.

Semiconductor/Electronics

We produce components for both Wafer Processing Front End and Test, Assembly, and Packaging on the Back End. With cutting-edge CNC machining, we meet the industry's most stringent tolerances.
